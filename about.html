Our Purpose

Reyes Holdings is dedicated to being the best in every segment in which we operate

Reyes Holdings is dedicated to being the best in every segment in which we operate, adding value to our customers’ supply chain and achieving unmatched value for our customers while creating an outstanding work environment for our employees and making a positive difference in our communities.

People Vision

Our vision is embracing our differences and similarities with a collective goal of striving for excellence in all that we do

Our team is a vibrant collection of great people built upon individual and diverse characteristics, values and beliefs , all of which are reflected across our enterprise

In our operations, in the way we treat each other, and in our service to our customers, suppliers, and business partners. We are committed to fostering an environment where all are valued, respected, and free to achieve their highest potential.

The Pillars of ourPeople Vision

  • Merit

    We provide a merit-based environment where we enable those who live our values to succeed

  • Individuality

    We value and respect the contributions of each individual team member to shape our enterprise success

  • Fairness

    We are committed to treating everyone fairly

Our Values

Excellence

Our enterprise is characterized by a commitment to excellence. Each day, all of us work together and strive to improve and be the best versions of ourselves for the benefit of the team.

People and safety

We value our people and their safety above all else. We know that great people are the key to continued success, and keeping our people healthy and safe is an essential component of that success.

Relationships

Our business is defined by great relationships. We seek to build and maintain great relationships with our teammates, our suppliers, our customers and our community.

Integrity

Integrity is the defining principle of our enterprise. We say what we mean and mean what we say – and we commit ourselves to upholding the highest standards of honesty and accountability.

Dedication
