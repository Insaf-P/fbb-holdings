47 yearsinbusiness

Founded in 1976 by two brothers, Chris and Jude Reyes, Reyes Holdings remains a family-run business and has become one of the largest privately-owned companies in America.

1976 - 1998
2005 - 2012
2014 - 2016
2017 - 2018
2019 - 2022
1976 - 1998

1976Dixie Systems is purchased in South Carolina

1989Expansion reaches the West Coast in California when Harbor Distributing is purchased

1998Reyes Holdings enters the food distribution industry with the acquisition of Martin Brower

2005 - 2012

2005Reyes Holdings expands when Reinhart Foodservice is purchased in Wisconsin

EARLY 2000’SDozens of food and beer distributors are acquired, fueling the largest growth period the company has ever seen

2012Reyes Holdings becomes the largest international distributor of McDonald’s with the acquisition of Keystone Foods into Martin Brower

2014 - 2016

2015Gold Coast Beverage is acquired in Miami, solidifying Reyes Beer Division as the largest beer distributor in the United States

2015Reyes Holdings officially enters the Coca‑Cola system with the granting of distribution territories in Chicagoland

2016Reyes Holdings ventures away from distribution for the first time and acquires nine Coca‑Cola production facilities in the Midwest

2017 - 2018

2017Reyes Holdings expands their Coca‑Cola reach in the largest acquisition to date with Reyes Coca‑Cola Bottling

2018Reyes Holdings expands their Martin Brower U.S. business with the acquisition of select Golden State Foods assets

2018Reyes Holdings enhances their market leading position in beer distribution with several acquisitions and expands into a new Santa Fe Springs facility

2019 - 2022

2019Reyes Beverage Group expands into Northern California with the addition of Golden Brands

2019Reyes Holdings grows their Coca‑Cola territory into Tennessee and Kentucky with the acquisition of Coca‑Cola Bottling Works of Tullahoma

2019After 15 successful years in business, Reinhart Foodservice was sold to fuel greater growth throughout the enterprise

2020 - 2021Reyes Beverage Group acquires many new distributors including their first territories in Indiana and Michigan as well as locations spanning the entire state of California

2022Our Coca-Cola companies combined to form a single business unit: Reyes Coca-Cola Bottling

Our eyes are always on the future

From our humble beginnings with a Schlitz beer distributorship, Reyes Holdings, LLC is now a global leader in the production and distribution of food and beverage products and one of the largest privately owned companies in America.
